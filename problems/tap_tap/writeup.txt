A quick google shows that the J-38 was a device used for Morse Code. We can plug our encrypted text into a Morse Code translator like this one:

http://cryptii.com/morsecode/text

It takes some fiddling, as the characters are not at first obvious. However, eventually the following setup is reached:

Separator:	=
Long:		_
Short:		-
Space:		[Arbitrary character]
